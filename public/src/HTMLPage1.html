Ms. Perfect : E09 - 03/27/2017

A Woman Who Has Been Living for Her Family Embarks on a Journey to Find Her True Self

As a mother of two children and a wife of a troublemaker husband, Shim Jae-bok finds it difficult to take her career to the next level. However, when her life becomes entangled with that of Lee Eun-hee, a woman who seems to have everything, things take an unexpected turn. As Jae-bok faces and overcomes one obstacle after another, she begins to rediscover her true self and femininity and strives to realize her potential as an individual, free of bondage as a mother and a wife.
